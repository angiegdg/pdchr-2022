Sultan Camp Biography
Sultan Camp is a recovering Military Headhunter, proud Shipbuilder and currently serves as the Director of the Hampton Roads Workforce Council’s (HRWC) Veterans Employment Centers, the Co-Chair of the Military and Veterans Executive Committee for Virginia Chamber of Commerce’s Blueprint 2030 and a Board Member for the Fort Eustis Civic Leaders Association (ECLA).
Kim Eley Biography
Specializing in personal development books and thoughtful children's books is the mission of Kim Wells Eley’s company, KWE Publishing; the common thread of both is to help people see what's possible and shift to a more enjoyable, fulfilled life. she says. As a writing coach and publishing consultant, Kim gives clients six steps to make their books a reality.

Kim is happily married to her BFF and has been for over 20 years. She’s a speaker; an author; a resident of Prince George, Virginia; a cat lover; a collector of orchids; and she gets all of her news from comedy shows.
Bryan Berry Biography
Bryan dealt with chaos in the investment advisory world for 30 years before one day hearing "you have 60 days to live" and managing the chaos that followed. He then chose to adjust his life's gameplay and now Bryan coaches leaders to lean into crises and harvest the chaos so that they can rise and accomplish their goals with passion and unwavering confidence. He loves to collaborate with others to help them turn chaos into an ally.
Lee Gimpel Biography
Gimpel is the founder of Better Meetings in Washington, DC. The firm helps organizations turn meetings from boring and unproductive to engaging and effective, be they staff meetings, board meetings, retreats, or annual conferences. His work blends meeting facilitation, audience engagement, collaborative innovation, and human-centered design.
Michael McCullough Biography
Michael is a Citizen Development Implementation Strategist who over the past 8 years has supported large project management offices by developing tools and processes which enable projects to be run more efficiently. His passion for innovation and problem solving has led him to embrace and become a global thought leader for Citizen Development.
Vonita Williams Biography
Vonita Williams has over 14 years in the training and development arena. She has experience in both the corporate and academic arenas and has a passion for improving situations.

Vonita also enjoys coaching, mentoring, event planning, and helping others reach their goals and potential.
Rodney Spears Biography
Rodney Spears is the CEO and President of Spears Consulting Group, LLC and is an enterprise data and analytics leader who maximizes the impact data & insights have on business results, operational efficiency & effectiveness, and creating a data-driven culture.

In addition to being a member of the Hampton Roads chapter of F3, Rodney supports several nonprofits, including the Roc Solid Foundation, HER Shelter, Tidewater Youth Services, and is a member of the Lefcoe Leadership Association. He’s also served as the Small Business Chair for the Armed Forces Communications and Electronics Association (AFCEA) Hampton Roads chapter.
Sonia Di Maulo Biography
Sonia Di Maulo, M.A., award-winning author, scholar, Certified Reinvention Practitioner, Harvest Performance, grounds emerging leaders, of all ages, to bring their purpose-driven ground-breaking ideas to life, to reinvent for results, so that every minute, every hour and every day is effortlessly designed to leave a positive impact.

End result? Leaders grounded in purpose and prosperity. chapter.
Donna Norman Biography
As your Certified Executive and Personal Development Coach, Donna L. Norman will help you leverage your innate skills to grow in your career or as an entrepreneur. She specializes in coaching strategies, equipping you with the tools needed to move forward and grow. Donna’s skill in developing talent has translated through the corporate world, higher education, and many budding entrepreneurs. In addition to an ICF ACC program coaching certification, she has a master’s degree in Organizational Learning and Leadership from The George Washington University. She is a wife and mother of three who enjoys reading books, playing board games with family and friends, and serving at church.
Nancy Gutierrez Biography
Nancy Gutierrez is an Enneagram teacher/coach who helps women discover their strengths, blind spots, and other habitual and often unconscious patterns of thinking, feeling, and behaving so they can stop living out of habit and start living on purpose!

At 50 years young, Nancy has created her dream job and is working completely in her element drawing from her natural abilities and decades-long experience as a leader, coach, mentor, teacher, and personal development junkie.
Dr. Anne Niccolli Biography
As a Training and Instructional Design specialist at the U.S. Coast Guard Leadership Development Center, I manage staff professional development and design leadership curricula for both classroom and remote learning.

My research seeks to uncover factors that affect cognitive processes and influence decisions. I teach graduate-level students as an adjunct lecturer at Eastern Connecticut State University and challenge students to explore and test theories for practical application.
Dr. Geoff Abbott Biography
Geoff is a 30-year Coast Guard veteran where he held two commands, led the Performance Excellence Program, and chaired the Commandant’s Innovation Council. An Excellence in Government (EIG) Senior Fellow and Partnership for Public Service Leadership Alumni Board member, he’s led several workshops on leading “from the middle.” Geoff’s book, Unauthorized Progress – Leading ‘from the Middle’ was a #1 Best-Seller in two Kindle categories. He currently is a federal agency senior leadership development faculty member.
Mark Bowers Biography
Mark Bowers helps do-gooders do more good. His specialty is in measuring the critical yet unquantifiable behaviors that drive outcomes. Mark has way too many years in leadership and management of government, commercial, and non-profit efforts. He also served twenty years as a US Navy submarine officer.

Mark’s current “labor of love” is as founder and executive director of the Professional Development Consortium of Hampton Roads which provides affordable, accessible, and intimate leadership education for membership associations.
